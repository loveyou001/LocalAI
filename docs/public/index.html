Peer 2 Peer

LocalAI - Run AI models locally with ease

LocalAI makes it simple to run various AI models on your own hardware. From text generation to image creation, autonomous agents to semantic search - all orchestrated through a unified API. LocalAI, created by Ettore Di Giacinto (mudler), is a Free and Open Source, community-driven project to make Free, Open AI accessible to everyone. The LocalAI stack is MIT licensed, and the models trained by LocalAI are available under the Apache 2.0 License.
